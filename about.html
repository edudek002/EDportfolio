Edyta graduated from the Warsaw University of Technology with the Master’s Degree in Engineering and from the University of Minnesota with the Master’s Degree in Math Education. For the past three years she led the Women in Science and Engineering program for the undergraduate S.T.E.M. female students at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC).



In 2014 she founded the Girls Who Code year-round program for 60 middle and high school girls at UIC. The goal of the program was to close the gender gap in Computer Science. She was also a Chicago Site Lead for six 7-week GWC Summer Immersion Programs. This year she successfully taught CSS, HTML and JavaScript to GWC students at the University of Minnesota (UMN). Recently Edyta attended the Full Stack Web Development Certificate program at UMN and is currently working as a Back End Developer.
