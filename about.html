In 2014 I founded a Girls Who Code program at the University of Illinois at Chicago. The goal of this program is to close a gender gap in Computer Science. I directed year-round activities for 60 middle and high school girls as well as two 7-week Summer Immersion Programs. This year I am leading an Intermediate level of the Girls Who Code program at the U of M.
